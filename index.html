Jenny grew up in Virginia and graduated with a degree in Physics from Longwood College (now Longwood University). While in school, she worked as an Office Assistant in a grocery store's accounting office and quickly became the Store Accountant. The field completely suits her problem-solving abilities and creativity, and even utilizes her understanding of data analysis from all those spreadsheets.

Harry Potter Trivia

Harry Potter Trivia

Date: February 23, 2019

Category: jQuery, Timers

Client:  Bootcamp Assignment

Live Demo

In this assignment, we were asked to create a trivia game using vanilla JavaScript and jQuery. Each question has a timer set to 15 seconds. If the user answers the question before the 15 seconds are up, they are told whether they were correct or not, and the quiz moves to the next question. If they do not answer the question within the 15 seconds, the correct answer is revealed and they are presented with the next question.
